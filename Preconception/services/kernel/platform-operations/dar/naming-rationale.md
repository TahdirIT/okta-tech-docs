# Platform Operations – Naming Rationale

## Reason for the Name
The service is named **Platform Operations** to accurately reflect its responsibility for operating and governing the platform itself, rather than managing a specific role or user interface.

## Why Not “Admin Management”
- *Admin* represents a role, not a platform capability.
- The service serves multiple internal teams (Operations, Support, Marketing, etc.).
- Role-based naming limits future scalability and clarity.

## Why “Platform Operations”
- **Platform**: Scope is platform-level, not tenant or end-user level.
- **Operations**: Focused on running, supervising, and coordinating platform activities.
- **Role-agnostic**: Supports multiple internal roles with different permissions.
- **Scalable**: Suitable for future expansion and reuse.

## Conclusion
**Platform Operations** was chosen as a neutral, scalable, and industry-aligned name that reflects the service’s true purpose without tying it to a specific role or interface.
